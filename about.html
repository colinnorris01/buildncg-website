Our Story

Building Dreams in the Queen City

At Norris Construction Group, we take pride in delivering high-quality construction and remodeling services to clients in Charlotte, NC and the surrounding areas. As a family-owned and locally operated company, we understand the importance of trust, transparency, and exceptional craftsmanship.

Our experienced team works collaboratively to bring your vision to life, ensuring attention to detail and exceptional craftsmanship in every project. From kitchen and bathroom renovations to custom home construction and outdoor living spaces, we treat every project as if it were our own home.

With a passion for what we do and a commitment to excellence, we specialize in creating breathtaking spaces that reflect the unique style and vision of each homeowner. Let us help transform your space today.
