How We Calculate Your Renovation Budget

Our budget calculator uses industry-standard guidelines trusted by contractors and real estate professionals across the Charlotte metropolitan area. For kitchen renovations, the National Association of Home Builders recommends allocating approximately 15% of your home's value. Bathroom remodels typically follow a 10% guideline. New construction in the Charlotte, NC market averages around $270 per square foot for quality custom home builds.

These are starting points — your actual investment depends on your design vision, material selections, and project scope.
